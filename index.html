About Me

Nicholas Bernhard is a writer, composer, and filmmaker. His films include Blackstone's Equation, a feature documentary about Colorado's worst wrongful conviction case. In 2017, he published the historical novel November in America, based on the Coal Strike of 1927. He has also written for Yellow Scene Magazine. Since 2016, he has been Exhibit Technician at the Miners Museum in Lafayette, Colorado.

Nicholas enjoys playing piano and bass, writing songs, and hiking. Some of his bass covers may be found here.
